Join us at our project meetings! It's great way to get immediately involved in working on projects and discussing potential solutions and problems. There is a place for everyone here from civil engineers to nurses. The best way to get involved is to email a subgroup lead or project lead to inquire how you may fit in the process. Then look over the resources online (see below) to educate yourself on the current progress of the project and show up and start contributing.

Come to our project meetings to get involved with our Peru project, Local Latrine project, Instrumentation group, or Fundraising group!

Mondays from 6:00 - 7:00 pm at the Austin Presbyterian Theological Seminary  (100 E. 27th Street, Austin, Texas 78705) McCord Community Center in room 204

 

CAMBIAR (Ancash, Peru) Project Meetings

The Peru Project team meets weekly to work on the design, planning and education for their irrigation project.
Project Leads:  Dacy Thomas, Natalia Corona, & Maria de la Fuente (peru@ewbgreateraustin.org)

 

Chacayá, Guatemala Project Meetings

The Guatemala Project team meets bi-weekly to work on the design, planning and education for their potable water project. You can join in by attending meetings or participating via web / conference call. Instructions for calling in are given below.

Instrumentation Group Meetings

The Instrumentation Group rotates between Peru and Chacaya meetings (and will be present at general meetings) to provide cost effective, simple, compact and contemporary solutions for the instrumentation needs of each project.

Project Lead: Anjan Contractor (anjancontractor@gmail.com)

 

Local Latrine Meetings

We've partnered with Farmshare Austin to assist in the design and implementation of a composting latrine for use at their facility.
