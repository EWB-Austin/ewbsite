What: The Water Trotter Urban Challenge is a fun and challenging urban trekking adventure over a distance of approximately three miles. Teams will navigate through the diverse urban ecosystem of Austin’s Waller Creek Corridor exploring local neighborhoods, the Capitol and the University of Texas at Austin campus. The urban challenge is designed to emphasize the importance of clean water in developing countries.

Impact: Your sponsorship of this event will help raise resources for EWB-Austin Chapter to construct vital water infrastructure programs in Peru and Guatemala.

When: April 25, 2015 at 10:00 AM

Where: Scholz Garten - 1607 San Jacinto

How to Participate: Each Team will consist of two participants.  At least one participant has to be over 18 years in age. Each participant will have to fill out a separate online form / application. The first member of a team can sign up and create a team from this website and the second team member can sign up by joining that team.

Sign up: https://wtuc2015-ewbga.eventbrite.com
