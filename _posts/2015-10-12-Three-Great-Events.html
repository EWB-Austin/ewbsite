Shoal Creek Clean Up

Adopt-a-Creek is a great initiative taken by one of our local non-profits, Keep Austin Beautiful, in an effort to keep our creeks clean from any littering, invasive plants, and hazardous pollution that effects the ecosystems.

Engineers Without Borders Greater Austin Chapter has adopted a creek that runs from shoal creek Blvd. till 29th street. On Saturday 10/17 we will be having our first clean-up day of the Fall semester. This a great volunteering event to give back to our beautiful city of Austin and a great opportunity to interact with fellow EWB members outside our general meetings setting.

We will be meeting at the parking lot off Shoal Creek Blvd, you can google map these coordinates to find the exact location: 30.292344, -97.749579

Please fill out the RSVP form if you plan on coming. See you all out there.
