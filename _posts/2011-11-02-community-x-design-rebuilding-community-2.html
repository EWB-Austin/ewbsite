Donations at the door to benefit the Central Texas  Wildfire Relief efforts. There will be a raffle for door prizes.

Presenters include:

EcoRise Youth Innovations
Partnership for a Sustainable World
Architects Without Borders - Austin
Latinos in Architecture
Design Voice
Engineers Without Borders - Austin
Austin Community Design and Development Center
Rainforest Partnership
